The Hard Truth

The Hidden Burden of Our Wardrobe

Our Belief

Every Old Thread is Not Waste

We believe every pre-loved thread holds the connection of creativity and life stories yet to be told. Under the artistic hands of Upcycling designers at ReFashion, ripped jeans and worn chinos are professionally cleaned, treated, and redesigned into premium, one-of-a-kind handcrafted fashion statements.

How It Works

The Circular Thread Journey

A 4-step closed-loop system – maximizing product lifespan, reducing water usage, and carbon emissions.

1. Pass Clothes
Pass on your pre-loved clothes to the circular ecosystem.
2. Earn GreenCoin
Earn green points for every eco-contribution.
3. Reborn (Upcycle)
ReFashion designers upcycle fabrics into unique pieces.
4. Green Shop
Enjoy handcrafted circular fashion tailored for Gen Z.
Reward System

Eco-Points Reward System

GreenCoin is the core connection of ReFashion's eco-community. By participating in environmental actions like donating clothes, shopping eco-friendly, or writing reviews, you earn GreenCoin. Eco-points can be redeemed for shopping discounts or donated to plant mangrove forests in Vietnam.
